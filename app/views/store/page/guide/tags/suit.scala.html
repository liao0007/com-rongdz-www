Trouser Fits

Classic Fit
Our most traditional fit, this style offers a higher rise along with the fullest seat, thigh, and leg of any of our trouser fits.
Tailored Fit
This updated look features a slightly lower rise with a trimmer seat, thigh, and leg than our Classic Fit.
European Fit
This contemporary look offers a modern cut with a low rise as well as a narrower fit through the seat, thigh, and leg than our Tailored Fit.
European Slim Fit
This is our slimmest and most fashion forward look, offering a more aggressive taper from knee to leg opening. It’s typically worn without a break.

Gold area represents the Classic Fit

Jacket Fits

Tailored Fit
This updated look features a silhouette that is slightly shorter and narrower than the Classic Fit.
European Fit
Our slimmest cut offers a fashion forward look and feel. It features narrow shoulders, chest, and sleeves as well as our shortest body length. It also has a close fit through the waist and hips. This fit is ideal for men with a lean silhouette.
Classic Fit
This traditional fit is familiar to most men and is suitable for all body types. It offers unrestrictive shoulders, chest, and sleeves, and has a standard body length.

Gold area represents the Classic Fit
